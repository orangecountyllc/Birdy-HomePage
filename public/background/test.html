Our Story

The Heart of Birdy

When I moved to a big city, I ditched my car—traffic, parking, and fines were just too much. Rideshare apps seemed like the answer, but I quickly noticed something was off. Drivers were overworked, underpaid, and the rider-driver connection felt cold and transactional. My uncle Tony, a limo driver, showed me a better way: real relationships, fair pay, and trust.

I learned drivers often keep less than $10 from a $20 fare, and riders—especially women—craved safer, familiar options. One driver in his 60s struggled to accept tips without cash or apps like Zelle. That sparked an idea: a digital business card for drivers to share their story, availability, and build trust with riders. That’s how Birdy took flight.

With Birdy, drivers create profiles to share who they are, and riders can choose someone they trust—not a random algorithm pick. It’s about fairness, familiarity, and freedom for both sides.

Why “Birdy”? I wanted something light and warm, like a friend you’d want to ride with. “Freedom” felt too heavy, but “Birdy” captures the ease and joy of connection. It’s about cutting out middlemen and bringing back the human touch to rides.

If you’ve ever wanted to ride with the same great driver again, Birdy’s here for you. Let’s make rides personal again—together.

– Jay, Founder

jay@getabirdy.com

Birdy lets drivers share their profile easily with passengers at the end of a ride. Just say, “Find me on Birdy at getbirdy.co/YourName.” Riders can view your background, social media, and story, building trust before booking you as their private driver. It’s a safer, more comfortable way to connect without sharing personal contact info.
