Privacy Policy

1. What We Do

We provide a platform where users (like drivers) can create digital business cards that include their contact info, vehicle details, and—if they choose—their real-time location. Riders and drivers use this info to connect, communicate, and arrange rides directly outside of our app.

2. What We Don’t Do

  • We do not arrange or manage rides.
  • We do not process payments.
  • We do not collect or store ride history.
  • We do not monitor your private communications.

3. Location Sharing

Location sharing is optional and controlled by the user. You decide when to turn it on or off. When active, your live location is only visible to users who view your profile while you're “online.” We do not track your background location or store location history.

4. What Data We Collect

  • Name
  • Contact method (phone, email, etc.)
  • Vehicle info (if submitted)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Live location (only if enabled)
  • Messages between users (temporarily, if chat is included)

5. How Your Data Is Used

Your information is used solely to display your profile, enable contact, and show live location if enabled. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

6. Your Responsibilities

This platform is intended for users who already know or have met each other through other means. By using this platform, you acknowledge that all users exchange digital business cards voluntarily. Any ride arrangements, communications, or payments happen independently and outside of our platform. We do not encourage or facilitate business between individuals who have not previously met or done business together.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but we cannot guarantee your safety during any interaction arranged through the app. Please use caution and good judgment.
